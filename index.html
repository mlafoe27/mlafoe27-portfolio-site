Nice to Meet You.

My name is Merritt La Foe, UX/UI Designer. Painter. Denver Resident. Beer enthusiast. Lover of all dogs. Foodie. Football Fan. Good tunes. Over the past five years, I worked as a Graphic Designer at a sports marketing corporation and as an Art Director for a commercial printing firm. I earned a BFA in Graphic Design and Painting emphases from Mississippi State University, and finishing up my UX/UI Certification from the University of Denver. I use empathy and adaptability in order to understand the user and their thought process. and feedback in order to create (and iterate) products that make sense and are enjoyable.
